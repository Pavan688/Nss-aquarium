Fishing for Tips

Many men go fishing all their lives without knowing that it is not fish they are after. - Henry David Thoreau

During the journey of my life, I have traveled to Australia, Nova Scotia, and Argentina. With each new location, I learned many lessons. I would like to share some of those lessons, specificaly about things to know before fishing. Here are some of the lessons:

  • Wear comfortable clothes and always account for weather.
  • Obtain all necessary permits a week ahead
  • Never forget First Aid and safety gear.
  • Research on seasonal fishes at the specific location.
  • Always eat before fishing starts.

Care for Tips

A part of taking care of your fishes includes keeping the aquarium clean. Here is a few key tips to keep aquarium clean.

  • Change water and clean gravel.
  • Rinse the filter.
  • Do not overfeed the fish.
  • Keep tank out of direct sunlight.
  • Wipe all the surfaces inside and out.

Another important fact to know is salinity of the water. I keep my Salinity at 1.026

For more informaton about salinity visit ratemyfishtank.com.
